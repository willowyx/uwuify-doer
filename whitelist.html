uwuify (whitelist)
whitelist sites from perma-uwu





Instructions:

Enter domains to whitelist, separated by commas (spaces and newlines are automatically ignored)
Pages and subdomains will be matched to more generic rules, but not the other way around: visiting example.com/page will match the rule for example.com
Regex and protocol specifiers (http/s) are not supported

Notes:

Applying the whitelist will overwrite all current entries, so input the entire list.
Whitelisted domains can still be manually uwuified but you'll be asked confirmation.
Entries are case-sensitive, generally use lowercase if you're unsure.
